The chart below shows the trends of 2013 digital music sales.

From the chart there is a noticable difference from the 2012 chart. The streaming sales of 2013 have gone up significantly, while ringtone sales have continued to decline. Another change that should be noted is that music download sales have slightly declined, showing that a change in the way music is received has changed.
